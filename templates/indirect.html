How do Indirect Donations work?

It's actually very simple. You either play a game, read something from our enormous collection of articles or try out some brain-tickling riddles. With the help of ads, all the revenue generated from them will be donated right away to the ones who need it. This makes it easier for users to help the needy without actually having to donate physical items. Be it 2 hours or 5 minutes, every bit of your time has a precious value.

You cannot do all the good that the world needs. But the world needs all the good that you can do!
